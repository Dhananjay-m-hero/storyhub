Explore the magical adventures of Ploofi, the mysterious Floating Isles, and the dark rise of Zysepteus.

🦄 Ploofi

Ploofi is a magical fluffy guardian who protects the Floating Isles. He collects magical feelings and unicorn horns. His powers include:


  • 🪽 Crystal Wings
  • 🌊 Underwater Breathing
  • 💖 Emotion Powers
  • ✨ Unicorn Horn Energy
  • 🌈 Light Magic

😈 Zysepteus

A soft but dangerous creature with red and purple fur. He possesses devil horns and searches for ancient Landstones. His goal is to corrupt the Floating Isles and gain ultimate power.

☁️ Floating Isles

The Floating Isles are ancient islands suspended in the sky. Half of the islands remain magical and beautiful. The other half has become corrupted by dark energy. Red and purple monsters roam the corrupted lands.

📖 Legend

Long ago, the Floating Isles were peaceful. When Zysepteus began collecting Landstones, darkness spread through the sky. Ploofi received the power of a magical Unicorn Horn and gained crystal wings. Together with Dhananjay, he must restore balance before the Isles are lost forever.
